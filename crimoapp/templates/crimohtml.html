Frequently Asked Questions

What is Crises Monitor?

+

Crises Monitor is a real-time platform that provides information and updates on natural disasters happening around the world, such as floods, earthquakes, wildfires, and more.

How do I receive alerts?

+

You will receive instant notifications on your device when a natural disaster occurs in your selected regions. You can customize these notifications to fit your preferences.

Can I track multiple disasters?

+

Yes, Crises Monitor allows you to track multiple disasters happening globally at once. You can filter by disaster type, region, and severity.

Is there any cost to using Crises Monitor?

+

No, Crises Monitor is completely free to use. You can access real-time disaster updates, statistics, and insights without any charge.

How accurate is the disaster data?

+

We use trusted sources and real-time data feeds from government agencies, international organizations, and news outlets to ensure the information is accurate and up-to-date.
